# Operator Pilot Plan

**iMOS 0.1.0-rc.1**  
**Build 024**

---

## Purpose

The iMOS operator pilot is a controlled daily-use period for the primary operator to validate:

1. The core vault workflow is productive and sustainable
2. Rosie's recommendations are useful and trustworthy
3. The backup and recovery system is dependable
4. The onboarding experience is complete and clear
5. Pilot measurements capture honest signal without noise

This is a **private, local pilot**. No data leaves the operator's device. No external service is contacted.

---

## Pilot Scope

| Activity | Required |
|----------|----------|
| Daily vault unlock and briefing | Yes |
| Morning brief review | Yes |
| Priority management | Yes |
| Commitment tracking | Yes |
| At least one decision record per week | Recommended |
| Evening reflection | Yes |
| Pilot feedback entry per week | Recommended |
| Recovery backup creation | Required before pilot begins |

---

## Pilot Duration

- **Minimum:** 14 days of active use
- **Recommended:** 30 days
- **Maximum before review:** 90 days

---

## Pilot Feedback

Use the **FEEDBACK** button in iMOS to record your observations after any notable Rosie interaction:

- Was the briefing or recommendation useful?
- Did it require significant mental effort to evaluate?
- Did Rosie make an incorrect assumption?
- Was important context missing?
- Did you have a concern about the accuracy or trustworthiness?

Feedback is stored in your encrypted vault only. It is never transmitted. It does not automatically change Rosie's behaviour. You remain in full control of any adaptation.

---

## During the Pilot

1. **Unlock your vault daily** — iMOS needs current data to be useful
2. **Record decisions** when you make significant choices — context matters
3. **Correct Rosie** when she is wrong — corrections make her more accurate
4. **Create backups weekly** — protect your data throughout the pilot
5. **Record feedback** after notable interactions — honest signal is more useful than positive signal

---

## At the End of the Pilot

Review the **Pilot Measurements** in the FEEDBACK panel:

- Briefing usefulness trend
- Number of corrections made
- Cognitive effort profile
- Daily workflow completion rate
- Backup readiness

Use these measurements to determine whether to proceed with production use, request adjustments, or extend the pilot.

## What the Pilot Does Not Do

- The pilot does not connect to any external service
- The pilot does not transmit your data
- The pilot does not rank or score you
- The pilot does not automatically change how Rosie works
- The pilot does not require any account or registration
