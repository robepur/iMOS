# Operator Onboarding Guide

**iMOS 0.1.0-rc.1**

---

## What is iMOS?

iMOS is your Individual Mission Operating System. It is a private, encrypted space to manage:

- **Priorities** — what matters most right now
- **Commitments** — things you have promised to do
- **Decisions** — choices you have made, with context
- **Reflections** — daily reviews of what you did and learned
- **Missions** — goal sequences with steps and evidence
- **Secrets** — encrypted credentials and private notes

Everything stays on your device. Nothing is shared without your permission.

---

## What is Rosie?

Rosie is your AI cognitive partner. She reads your local data to:

- Present a **daily briefing** summarising your state
- **Surface patterns** she observes across your activity
- **Make bounded recommendations** when she sees something worth addressing
- **Explain her reasoning** so you can evaluate it

Rosie does not act autonomously. Every recommendation requires your explicit decision.

---

## You Are Always in Control

iMOS never changes anything without your confirmation. Rosie observes and advises. You decide whether to:

- Accept a recommendation
- Reject it
- Correct Rosie when she is wrong

Your corrections are recorded. Rosie does not repeat mistakes you have flagged.

---

## Your Vault

Your vault is encrypted using AES-256-GCM. Your passphrase is never stored or transmitted. If you lose it, the vault cannot be recovered — by you or by iMOS.

**Choose a strong passphrase** (at least 12 characters). Write it down somewhere safe and separate from your device.

---

## Your Daily Workflow

1. Open iMOS and unlock your vault
2. Review the **Arrival** screen
3. Begin your **Morning Brief**
4. Work through your **Focus** session
5. Close the day with an **Evening Reflection**
6. Your data persists automatically

---

## Creating a Recovery Backup

Before you finish onboarding, create a recovery backup. Go to **VAULT → Recovery Console → Export Backup**.

Store the backup file somewhere separate from your device. You will need it if you change browsers, clear your storage, or lose access to your device.

---

## Correcting Rosie

Open the **ROSIE** panel. Find the understanding that is wrong. Click **Correct** and describe what is actually true.

Your correction is stored in your vault. Rosie will not make the same mistake again. You can review all her understandings at any time in the ROSIE panel.

---

## Synchronisation

Synchronisation across devices is **disabled by default**. It is an optional future capability that requires your explicit consent and a supported provider. iMOS works completely offline without it.

---

## Reviewing This Guide

Open **VAULT → Onboarding Guide** to review any of these steps at any time.
